FAQ

Report Issue
How does the wallet work?▾
Add money using UPI, cards, or net banking. Your balance is deducted per call minute and gifts sent.
What are the call charges?▾
Each host sets their own rate. Audio calls start from ₹2/min and video calls from ₹3/min.
How do I become a host?▾
Tap "Become a Host" on the login or profile page, complete your profile, and submit for review. Approval takes 24–48 hours.
Can I get a refund?▾
Unused wallet balance is refundable within 7 days of recharge. Completed call charges are non-refundable.
Why does my call disconnect?▾
Calls disconnect when your balance runs low or due to poor internet. Keep your wallet topped up for uninterrupted calls.
How do gifts work?▾
Send virtual gifts during calls. The cost is deducted from your wallet and hosts earn a percentage of each gift value.
